SAGITTARIUS

Information

Sagittarius (♐︎) (Greek: Τοξότης, romanized: Toxótēs, Latin for "archer") is the ninth astrological sign, which is associated with the constellation Sagittarius and spans 240–270th degrees of the zodiac. Under the tropical zodiac, the sun transits this sign between approximately November 22 and December 21. Greek mythology associates Sagittarius with the centaur Chiron, who mentored Achilles, a Greek hero of the Trojan War, in archery. Sagittarius, the half human and half horse, is the centaur of mythology, the learned healer whose higher intelligence forms a bridge between Earth and Heaven. Also known as the Archer, Sagittarius is represented by the symbol of a bow and arrow.

How it formed

Sagittarius is a constellation in the night sky, which was identified and named by ancient Greek astronomers based on the pattern of stars they observed. The pattern of stars in the constellation resembled a centaur with a bow and arrow, like the mythological character of Chiron. Over time, the constellation was associated with the astrological sign and typical characteristics were assigned to people born under this sign based on the mythological attributes of the centaur. The formation of the astrological sign Sagittarius is thus based on the observed pattern of stars and the mythology associated with it.

Where and when to find Sagittarius

Sagittarius can be found in the southern sky between the constellations of Scorpius and Capricornus. It is best viewed in the late summer and early winter months, particularly during November and December when it is at its highest point in the sky. The exact time and location to view Sagittarius may vary depending on your location and time zone. You can use a star chart or astronomy app to help you locate Sagittarius in the night sky.

Fun fact !

Sagittarius is the ninth astrological sign in the zodiac and is represented by the archer. It is said to be one of the most adventurous and curious signs. Sagittarius is ruled by the planet Jupiter, which is associated with growth, prosperity, and abundance. This sign is known for its optimistic, enthusiastic, and open-minded nature. People born under this sign are often described as one-of-a-kind, free-spirited individuals who love to explore and take risks.
